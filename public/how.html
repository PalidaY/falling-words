"Raining Word" is a fun experience of the word typing game that you need to type the word as fast as possible!!! within the time limit. You can play with your friend too by joining in the same room. Words will falling from the top to the bottom and disappear as the one of the players finish typing the word.


GAME MODES:


1.EASY = 3 sec per word


2.MEDIUM = 2 sec per word


3.HARD = 1.2 sec per word


4.SUPER HARD = 1 sec per word

Types of words:

    General Words: +10 points

    Long Words: +50 points


5.WORD TRAP = 3.5 sec per word

Types of words:

    Category Words(Category=Country): +10 points

    Out of Category Words: -10 points

    Forbidden Words: score turn to 0

6.WAR = 2.6 sec per word


score start at 500 points


Types of words:

    General Words: +10 points

    Long Words: +50 points

    Forbidden Words: score turn to 0

    Angel Words: increase your opponent score by 50 points

    Weapon Words: attack your opponent (and maybe yourself) base on the weapon word

      Nuclear: all players score turn to 0

      Missile: -100 points from your opponent

      Bomb: -50 points from your opponent

      Snipers, Rifle, Pistols, Gun, Shotgun, Handguns: -25 points from your opponent

      Sword, Knife: -10 points from your opponent
